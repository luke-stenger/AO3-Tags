Welcome!

Prepare to embark on a wonderful journey of male/male fiction! Building off of our class's collaborative definition of BL, this websites aims to quantitatively measure which story elements are more associated with male/male (M/M) stories. We do this by analyzing data from the online fanfiction repository Archive of Our Own (AO3). Please navigate by clicking on the links in the gray bar above this paragraph.

In Background, you will learn a little bit about the design of the AO3 website, and why it is such a M/M haven. In Goals / Procedere, you'll hear about exactly how we can use AO3's data to find connections between M/M stories and certain tropes. The most interesting page is Results: you'll get to see exactly which elements are disproportionately common in M/M stories. Finally, in Conclusions you'll hear about the limitations of this analysis, but why it is ultimately valuable for studying genres like BL.
